Welcome to India International School Alumni!

The India International School’s Alumni Panel is a recognition opportunity for alumni students to stay connected with fellow students, faculty and other alumni colleagues. At IIS, we are committed to providing academic growth and overall development to our students by helping them identify their academic goals. We are very proud of our students on securing places in some of the high ranked universities across the world.

The Alumni Panel of IIS always looks for ways to strengthen connections between the school and the Alumni network. With the help of these initiatives, we can foster an enthusiastic and strong global community that helps Alumni and their peers maintain a beneficial relationship.

The Alumni Panel helps in:

  • Fostering healthy relationships with alumni
  • Enabling alumni connections for personal growth and development
  • Creating a spirit of loyalty and involvement among alumni students
