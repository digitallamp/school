Welcome to the IIS Bangalore Portal

The day scholar cum residential school has earned a reputation as one of the premier institutions that offers a blend of global education under the shade of Indian values.

We have crafted a forte in holistic academics that spans multiple accreditations

  • Indian Certificate of Secondary Examination or ICSE
  • International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme or IBDP
  • International General Certificate of Secondary Education or IGCSE
  • Indian School Certificate or ISC

The broad choice of educational avenues makes it a perfect foundation for your child’s success in future vocations and in life.
