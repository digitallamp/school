Words of Inspiration

MR.KRISHNA, K.A.S.
CHAIRMAN

He is the founder Chairman of the India International School. An educationalist, Who has been instrumental in setting up the school. Having served in the Government of Karnataka in several key positions he always nurtured a longing to give back to the society .In 2002 he set up the India International School with an intention to ensure academic excellence and to open doors to the best universities abroad.

Going forward with his innate philanthropic interests to pledge his all towards education, he is in the process of developing a smart village with inputs of The Indian Institute of Science. Besides a School the village would boast of a Community Health Centre, Safe drinking water , Solar Energy and Vocational Training facilities

Chairmain Speech at Pre Activity & Pre Primary Annual Day

MRS. PADMAVATHI KRISHNA
CEO

“Welcome to the new school year ! Our commitment at IIS is to provide a safe and intellectually challenging environment that will empower students to become innovative thinkers, creative problem solvers and inspired learners prepared to thrive in the twenty-first century.

It is with pride that we hold high our standards and ask each of you to continue the extraordinary record of our school. I wish you to graduate out of IIS as good, human & responsible global citizens.”

Mrs.Latha Gowda
Principal
M.Sc, M.Phil, B.Ed, SLET, IBDP CAT-1 in Biology and ESS

The Institution stands at the frontiers of global innovation in academics as well as all the other components that make education whole. I invite you to peruse the extensive range of resources and opportunities that we offer to every student at India International School. From classrooms to sports and wellness, the amenities are both plenty and of international standards. Most important , the teaching and non-teaching staff at the Institution combine in them the calibre and culture to help students fulfill their higher education goals.
